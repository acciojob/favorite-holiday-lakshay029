Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station in Himachal Pradesh known for its snow-covered mountains, scenic valleys, adventure sports, and peaceful atmosphere.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Solang Valley - Famous for adventure sports.
  2. Rohtang Pass - Known for snow-covered mountains.
  3. Hadimba Temple - A historic and peaceful temple.
  4. Old Manali - Popular for cafes and culture.
  5. Beas River - Great for river rafting.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu - Traditional Himachali bread.
  • Thukpa - Tibetan noodle soup.
  • Momos - Popular steamed dumplings.
  • Babru - Himachali snack.
  • Trout Fish - Famous local dish.
