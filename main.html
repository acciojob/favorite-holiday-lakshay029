Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station in Himachal Pradesh known for its snow-covered mountains, scenic valleys, adventure sports, and peaceful atmosphere. It is one of the most popular tourist destinations in India.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Solang Valley - Famous for adventure activities like paragliding and skiing.
  2. Rohtang Pass - Known for its breathtaking snow views.
  3. Hadimba Temple - A historic temple surrounded by cedar forests.
  4. Old Manali - Popular for its cafes, culture, and relaxing vibe.
  5. Beas River - Perfect place for river rafting and sightseeing.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu - A traditional Himachali bread.
  • Thukpa - A delicious Tibetan noodle soup.
  • Trout Fish - Fresh and flavorful local specialty.
  • Momos - Popular steamed dumplings.
  • Babru - A tasty Himachali snack similar to kachori.
